About Us

With nearly 30 years of experience in heavy hydraulic vehicle repair, our mechanics have mastered everything from hydraulic system overhauls to precision transmission work on dumper trucks, tow trucks, and car transporters. Led by our founder, Iqbal Rana, their unmatched expertise and dedication ensure that every vehicle receives top-quality service, backed by decades of proven skill and customer trust.
